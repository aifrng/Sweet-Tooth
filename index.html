Sweet Tooth Story

Sweet Tooth Pastries began in a small home kitchen in Cavite, where every cake was baked with patience, passion, and a love for simple joys. What started as baking for family and friends slowly grew into a dream of sharing handcrafted desserts with the community. Inspired by Filipino flavors and classic French techniques, each creation blends comfort and elegance. Today, Sweet Tooth Pastries continues that tradition—turning everyday moments into something a little sweeter, one cake at a time.
